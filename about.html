About Our Initiative

Learn about our mission to protect and preserve harbour ecosystems

Who We Are

Healthy Harbour Watchers is a community-driven initiative dedicated to monitoring, protecting, and preserving the health of our harbour waters. Founded by a group of passionate environmental advocates, we bring together scientists, volunteers, and concerned citizens to ensure our harbours remain vibrant and sustainable.

Our History

Established in 2020, our organization was born out of concern for declining water quality and marine biodiversity in local harbour areas. What started as a small group of volunteers conducting weekly water tests has grown into a comprehensive monitoring and advocacy program that engages hundreds of community members.

What We Do

Our work encompasses several key areas:

  • Water Quality Monitoring: Regular testing of pH levels, dissolved oxygen, nutrient concentrations, and pollutant detection
  • Marine Life Surveys: Tracking species diversity, population health, and habitat conditions
  • Community Education: Workshops, school programs, and public events to raise awareness about harbour health
  • Policy Advocacy: Working with local government to implement sustainable harbour management practices
  • Cleanup Initiatives: Organizing regular harbour cleanup events to remove debris and pollutants

Our Values

We are guided by principles of scientific rigor, community engagement, environmental stewardship, and transparency. We believe that healthy harbours are essential for thriving coastal communities and that everyone has a role to play in protecting these vital ecosystems.

Join Our Team

Whether you're a scientist, student, or simply someone who cares about our environment, there's a place for you in Healthy Harbour Watchers. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels to participate in our monitoring programs, educational initiatives, and community events.
